Registration Fees

Author/Participant Registration fees

1.      Participant Registration fees

a.      Rs.2500 – for faculty members

b.      Rs.2000 – for Students/Research Scholars/OITS members/IEEE members

c.       Rs.3,500 – for industry participants

d.      $100 USD – for participants from outside India

           Registration includes the standard registration kit.

2.      Author Registration fees

a.      Rs.7000 – for faculty members

b.  Rs. 6000 - for IEEE members

c.      Rs.5000 – for students/Research Scholars/OITS members
