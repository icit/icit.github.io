Reaching the city



Bhubaneswar, the capital city of Orissa state is located in the East part of India and is well connected through Air, Rail and Road Network.


Air Connection
The Biju Patnaik International Airport Bhubaneswar is well connected to all airport of different cities.
Rail Connection
The Bhubaneswar city (station code bbs) is well connected by Indian railway to major cities of India. To get the list of trains and their schedules, please visit www.indianrail.gov.in. Participants can also book their tickets online at www.irctc.co.in.
Road Connection
Bhubaneswar is well linked to the rest of India by the national highways. The Bus Stand of Bhubaneswar at Baramunda is located on NH5.
