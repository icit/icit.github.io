Venue

WELCOME to

13th  International Conference on Information Technology

ICIT (International Conference on Information Technology) is a premier international conference and forum for high quality research in the area of Information Technology. ICIT2014 is organized by Silicon Institute of Technology (http://www.silicon.ac.in) in collaboration with Orissa IT Society (http://www.oits.org). This will be held at Bhubaneswar, India from 22nd-24th December 2014. Researchers, developers, and practitioners from academia and industry are invited to present their research findings on various topics related to Information Technology and its Applications.
