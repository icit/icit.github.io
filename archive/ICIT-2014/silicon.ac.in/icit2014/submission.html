Paper submission and deadlines


Website:
All the papers need to be submitted through the Easychair https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icit2014

Important dates:
Papers due: 25th June, 201415th July, 2014, 11:59:59pm IST
Acceptance Notification: 25th August, 2014
Camera-ready: 17th Oct (Friday), 2014  


Format:

The manuscripts need to be formatted as a double column conference format (http://www.computer.org/portal/web/cscps/formatting). The maximum length of the manuscript needs to be of 6-pages. All submitted papers will undergo DOUBLE-blind-review by the program committee. Authors need to prevent identity disclosure by: (1) not listing names and affiliations of the authors on the manuscript, (2) not saying "my work" or "our work" in the text while citing self-references, and (3) not writing acknowledgments in such a way that identity of authors are implied. Author information should only be included in the submission form. Manuscripts not following this requirement or exceeding 6-page length will be rejected without review. Authors need to submit a single pdf file of their manuscripts at: https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icit2014


Author's Final Paper Formatting and Submission Instructions webpage (Online Author Kit) is available at the following:

http://www.ieeeconfpublishing.org/cpir/authorKit.asp?Facility=CPS_Dec&ERoom=ICIT+2014
